The Core

Your core group. Five to seven people. Every month. High-trust.

The foundation of DC BLACK is a small, carefully matched mastermind. Five to seven members, grouped by industry, stage, and multiple other factors based on your goals. You meet monthly for two hours with the group led by a facilitator.

Each session runs a hot seat where members bring their current challenge or learning, the group acts as a peer advisory board. The feedback is direct and experienced — from people who are taking an active role in understanding you and your business. Between calls, the group stays connected on chat and email.

One month someone's weighing a PE firm offer. The next, they're figuring out how to step back while keeping equity. The month after, it's an operations manager who's become the bottleneck, or a partner dynamic that's quietly breaking things. We all work together and share resources to overcome our biggest challenges together.

The Rule

One rule makes everything else possible.

No pitching. No selling to other members. No sliding into someone's DMs with an offer.

It sounds simple, but it changes everything. When nobody has an agenda, the feedback is honest. When you bring your hardest problem to the group, you get direct answers — not a soft lead-in to a sales conversation. Members show up to each other's hot seats and walk away with as much value as the person in the chair.

DC BLACK works because of what it isn't. It's not a network event. It's not a Slack group. It's not another place to build an audience. It's a small, global community of founders who actually know each other — and show up for each other the same way.

Masterminds are monthly calls (2–2.5 hours long) led by an experienced facilitator. You'll be joined by 4–6 other DC Black members, grouped by specific interests and industries. While masterminds can be an extremely valuable use of your time, they are not mandatory as we recognize they don't fit everybody's schedule or lifestyle. You can opt in for a membership without a mastermind.
