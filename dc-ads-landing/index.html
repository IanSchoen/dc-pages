What's included

Built around how founders actually work.

Everything in the DC is designed around one thing: peer-to-peer connection between people who run real businesses.

💬
On-demand peer advice
Post a problem in any channel and get answers from people who've actually solved it. Hiring, pricing, partnerships, exits — no gurus, no upsells.
🎯
Mastermind groups
Get matched into a small group of 4–6 peers at your revenue stage. Regular calls, real accountability, specific to your business situation.
🌏
Global events
DCBKK (Bangkok), DC Mexico, DC Week, and local Juntos every year. In 25 years of business, members call DCBKK the best event they've ever attended.
🔒
Private by design
Curated and closed since 2011. Members share revenue numbers, equity structures, and real challenges because the community isn't public.
📍
Local meetups
Combat isolation and build your core support group through member-led Juntos and city-based groups — wherever in the world you're based.
🤝
A network that means something
The DC is built on connections. Business partnerships, hiring referrals, travel recommendations — the relationships here are real and lasting.
